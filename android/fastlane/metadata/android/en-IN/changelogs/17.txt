EcoOne has updated new and helpful functionalities for Ecopark residents, especially during the difficult time of Covid-19. From now on, residents and frequent visitors who are using Ecobus everyday are able to manage their family members' bus cards, extend bus cards directly through EcoOne. We hope that this updated version will bring to you the best experiences while using Ecobus.
